Notes to Grader:
For Asgn3B Requirements: Implemented camera movement with WSAD, and rotation with Q & E. Has
perspective camera implemented, and a world is implemented to walk around in. You can add and
delete blocks. Added a simple story to the world where a bunch of knights are lounging in a
courtyard. Also added a simple game to find the gold block. Also meets performance requirements.

Additional Features: Implemented collision detection. If you try running into the boundary (stone wall),
you will be prevented from crossing it. Also, I added my blocky animal to the world. Furthermore, I
added buttons on the webpage to choose the material for placing a block.

GAME INSTRUCTIONS: Find randomly generated golden block, walk close to it, and click it to win.
An alert pops up when found and clicked.
Controls: To DELETE a block, left click and to ADD a block press "x"
Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
