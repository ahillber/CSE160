Notes to Grader:
For Asgn4 Requirements: There is at least 1 cube (1pt) and there is a sphere (1pt). The
lighting (ambient, diffuse, specular) works correctly and you can change color with slider (2.5pts).
There is a small cube to act as visual marker for light (0.5pts). There is button to turn light
on and off (1pt). The light moves around the world, and can also be moved with sliders (1pt).
The light works with 3D world and my blocky animal (0.75pts). There is a button to visualize
normals with color (1pt). Site link posted in canvas comments (0.5pts).

Notes: Light orbits in a circle by default. Use the buttons to stop the animation, then use sliders.
Controls: WSAD (movement) Q&E (turn camera). X (place block) left-click (destroy block)
Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
